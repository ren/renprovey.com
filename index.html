1 Hour / Day

The embarrassing thing for me is that I've had this book for 8 months, but have mainly been using it as a reference, a quick lookup here, a quick check there. So as I read a bit each day, I'll be posting the bits and pieces here that help fill in my knowledge gaps of git.
